Hi, I'm Melanie, a Ruby Dev, blogger, and runner from Seattle, WA.

In the Spring of 2014, my husband and I packed up our lives, rented out our house in Seattle, and kicked off a year of traveling the world. Over the past year, I've discovered my passion for writing, nourished my love of learning, and have become a productivity ninja.

Currently, we're based in the beautiful town of Sevilla, Spain, where we spend our days working from the sunny apartment we found on Airbnb. I enjoy fiddling with exercism.io, working with various APIs, and working on a handful of Rails side projects.

My travel blog, (aptly) named We've Made a Huge Mistake, chronicles our journey around the world. Fair warning: I've occasionally been known to use sarcasm.
